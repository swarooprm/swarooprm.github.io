Swaroop Mishra

I have been involved in the full development lifecycle of language models for several years, encompassing pre-training, post-training, data quality, human data collection, instruction following, reasoning capabilities, model architecture design, and agent development. Over the last 15 years, my research experience has spanned more than 10 industries and research institutions across various roles, including research scientist, software engineer, and technical consultant.

I am a Research Scientist at Google DeepMind (Ex-Google Brain), where I work on Gemini reasoning. I built a System that got some crucial points in the International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO) 2024 and critically contributed to our Silver Medal along with AlphaProof and AlphaGeometry. I also co-led reasoning research that contributed to various successful launches SOTA Gemini model including the popular Google IO 2024 launch. Most of my patented works at Google revolves around LLM Reasoning, Scaling, Post-Training, Self-Improvement and Inference.

My main research contributions (in the form of publicly available papers) are

  • Instruction-tuning: Pioneering work: the first paper on instruction-tuning, Scaling: Super-NaturalInstructions, Applications: Biomedical Instruction-tuning, Aspect Based Sentiment Analysis, Evaluation: Instruction-following Evals, instruction-example equivalence and Bias: instruction-bias (EACL 2023 Outstanding Paper Award)
  • Reasoning: Methods: reframing, question-decomposition, math via programs, help me think, learn to explain, take a step back, selective answering, summary of thought and Dataset: NumGLUE, FeasibilityQA and TarGEN
  • Model Self-Improvement: Training Method: Self-Instruct, Evaluation Method: Self-Discover and Limitation: Self-Correction Limitations.
  • New Products and Beyond: User-friendly and controllable LLMs for structured thoughts and personalized model responses and other patented works.
