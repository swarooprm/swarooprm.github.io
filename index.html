Swaroop Mishra

I am a Research Scientist at Google DeepMind (Ex-Google Brain), where I work on Bard and other AI products. I wrote the first paper on instruction-tuning, developed various prompt-engineering methods e.g. reframing, and identified program representation to better do mathmeatical reasoning. I won the ASU 2023 Dean's Dissertation Award and CEN CS PhD Outstanding Student Award. I am also a proud alumni of IIT Kanpur.

Email  /  Google Scholar  /  Semantic Scholar  /  Linkedin  /  Twitter

Research

My main focus area is Learning from Instructions/Prompting. I am also excited about related areas such as Quantitative Reasoning, Efficient ML & NLP, Robustness & Generalization, Selective Answering, Spurious Bias and ML & NLP Applications. My strength lies in generating new ideas and I am fortunate to collaborate with a diverse set of awesome researchers through my ideas.
